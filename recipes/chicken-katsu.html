Ingredients

  • Chicken: preferably already dead(humanely), boneless, skinless and breast halves
  • Seasonings: go wild
  • Flour: this sticks to the chicken
  • Egg: this sticks to the flour
  • Panko: this will stick to the egg, mmmm crispy breadcrumbs
  • Oil: dangerous stuff

Steps

  1. Season the chicken
  2. Cover it in flour
  3. Drench it in egg
  4. Coat it in panko
  5. Fry till golden brown
  6. Remember! S.C.D.C.F!
