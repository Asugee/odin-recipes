Ingredients

  • Chopped baby carrots
  • Frozen green peas
  • Vegetable oil
  • Minced garlic
  • Eggs
  • White rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Assemble the ingredients
  2. Boil the carrots, for a while and stir in peas, immediately after drain in a colander
  3. Heat wok over high heat, pour in vegetable oil
  4. Stir in carrots, peas and garlic
  5. Add eggs and quickly stir with vegetables
  6. Stir in rice, add soy sauce and stir rice to coat, drizzle with sesame oil and toss again.
  7. Plate Up!
